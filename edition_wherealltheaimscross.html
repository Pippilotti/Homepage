where all the aims cross

where all the aims cross
2018
cotton, polyamid, lycra
edition 276 + 4 e.p. + 2 a.p.
Jahresgabe 2018, Kunsttreff 13

The edition that can be worn is asking about the possibility of a conjoint utopian reality. When the wearers – which are members of a monthly gathering group – meet, a possible imaginary dynamic network is evolving in the space. The prolongations of the arrows from everybody‘s contradictory feet cross eventually at different places and visualise the point where everybody‘s desires congregate. Whenever one moves, their desires move with them. The piece functions as a map for a collective utopian.
