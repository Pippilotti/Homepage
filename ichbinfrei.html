Ich bin frei und mir ist schlecht

2023
Newspaper
Released in September 2023
Concept, texts and drawings: Tanja Schwarz & Olivia Abächerli
Graphic Design: Viola Zimmermann & Loris Aregger
Language: German
Dimensions: 35 x 50cm, 24 pages
Edition: 300

The newspaper is an insight to a dialogue between Tanja Schwarz and Olivia Abächerli that started in 2021 and consists of both texts and drawings that have been sent back and forth.
