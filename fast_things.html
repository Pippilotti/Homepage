Fast things

2013 – 2017
mixed media

Between cosmos and cabinet, raw, full of banalities of yesterday afternoon - in opposition to questions about the new world. It is formulated interests, being expressed or whispered fast or slowly in a certain language. The private language is thereby moving towards its own limits. The pieces are hinged between intimacy and its outside. The accumulation of more than 800 rapidly evolved drawings, paintings and objects make up one complete work. A small selection is shown here.
