MX3000

2019
video installation
HD, 16:9, 09:25, stereo, loop
@Sattelkammer, Bern, CH

The animation – projected on the inside of the space's tiny bathroom's door – comments on the exhibition's ("Don't look back in anger") emphasis of a (non?-)political spiritual hedonism and claims to function as an empty manifesto, a satisfying commodity, slightly activating nonpolitical affectivity. It is thereby a contraparty watching you and mirroring you as a subject within the unknown capitalist future.
