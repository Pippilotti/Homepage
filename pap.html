P. A. P.

2018
2 laser prints
895 x 1280 mm

Not having understood why a father planted sticky nettles in the garden, this project is dedicated to brimstones, a sort of butterflies, still existing, in need for nettles in order to survive. The posters show the advertisement of advertisements: Imagine a nettles park, a habitat for brimstones and a safe space.
